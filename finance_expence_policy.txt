--- Finance and Expense Policy ---

**I. Expense Report Submission:**
All expense reports must be submitted through the Expensify portal within **30 days** of the purchase or the completion of the trip. Reports submitted after 60 days may be subject to denial.

**II. Travel and Meals:**
Employees traveling for business are eligible for per diem rates based on the travel location. The maximum daily limit for meals is $75 in domestic cities and $120 in international cities. Receipts are mandatory for all expenses over $25.

**III. Software and Equipment Purchasing:**
Software licenses costing under $500 can be purchased directly by the employee and reimbursed via an expense report. Purchases over $500 must be submitted as a purchase order (PO) request to purchasing@company.com with the department head's approval attached. Use cost center **MKT-501** for Marketing expenses and **HR-100** for Human Resources expenses.

**IV. Reimbursement and Payment:**
Reimbursements are processed bi-weekly via direct deposit. Any expense disputes should be directed to the Accounts Payable team at extension 700.
