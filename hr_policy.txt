--- HR Policy and Employee Handbook Summary ---

**I. Leave and Time Off Policy:**
All full-time employees are eligible for 15 paid vacation days per year. Sick leave is unlimited but requires a doctor's note after 3 consecutive days. New employees must complete a 90-day probationary period before requesting vacation time. All vacation requests must be submitted through the internal HR portal (portal.company.com/hr).

**II. Benefits and Wellness:**
The company provides health, dental, and vision insurance. Enrollment is managed through the benefits team. Employees are eligible for a $50 monthly wellness reimbursement for gym memberships or fitness classes.

**III. Performance and Growth:**
Annual performance reviews are conducted every November, with mid-year check-ins in June. Employees are encouraged to discuss training budgets with their direct managers.

**IV. Contact Information:**
For urgent policy questions, contact the HR department directly at extension 400 or via email at hr@company.com.**V. Hiring and Termination:**
The standard employment contract is at-will. All new hire onboarding documentation must be completed within the first three days via the HR portal. In the event of voluntary termination, employees are required to provide a minimum of two weeks' notice. The final paycheck will include accrued, unused vacation time.
